Seeds?

Each brown “seed” is not a seed, but actually a “complete” flower, with all four reproductive parts.

Petal?

One yellow “petal”, as we know it to be, is not a petal but a flower that is sterile, or “incomplete.”

Birds & Bees

Flowers have four reproductive parts (carpal, sepal, pistil, petal). “Complete” flowers have all four, parts and “incomplete” flowers are missing some.

One Flower?

“One” sunflower is not a single flower at all, but in fact a whole bouquet!
